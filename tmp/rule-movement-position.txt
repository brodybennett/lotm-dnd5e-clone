Movement and Position Movement in sequence play keeps the core turn structure, but character speed gains a pathway-derived edge so higher-sequence characters are mechanically faster. Final Speed = Base Movement - Exhaustion Reduction + Movement Bonus + Pathway Mobility Edge - Instability Penalty Pathway Mobility Edge Pathway Mobility Edge (feet) = 5 * clamp(Tier + floor((Potency - 2) / 3), 0, 6) Sequence 9-8: typically +0 ft Sequence 7-6: typically +5 ft Sequence 5-4: typically +10 to +15 ft Sequence 3-2: typically +15 to +25 ft Sequence 1-0: typically +25 to +30 ft The edge applies to each movement mode the character already has (walk, climb, swim, fly, burrow). It does not grant a movement mode by itself. Instability Penalty Corruption 0-49: 0 ft penalty Corruption 50-74: 5 ft penalty Corruption 75-99: 10 ft penalty Corruption 100+: 15 ft penalty This keeps sequence progression meaningful while preserving corruption pressure and existing armor/encumbrance constraints. Baseline floor: if a character has no species/race speed assigned, walk speed defaults to 30 ft before sequence modifiers.
